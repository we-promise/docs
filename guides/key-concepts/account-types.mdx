# All Account Types Sure Offers

Sure supports a comprehensive range of account types to track your complete financial picture. This page provides a complete reference of all available account types and their subtypes.

## Overview: Assets vs. Liabilities

| Classification | Types | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Assets** | Cash, Investment, Crypto, Property, Vehicle, Other Asset | Things you own |
| **Liabilities** | Credit Card, Loan, Other Liability | Things you owe |

Anything of value that doesn't fit other categories:

## Liability Accounts

### Credit Card

Track credit card balances and spending.

**Additional fields:**

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| APR | Annual Percentage Rate |
| Available Credit | Remaining credit limit |
| Minimum Payment | Minimum amount due |
| Annual Fee | Yearly card fee |

### Loan

Track mortgages, auto loans, student loans, and other installment debt.

| Subtype | Short Name | Full Name |
| --- | --- | --- |
| mortgage | Mortgage | Mortgage |
| student | Student | Student Loan |
| auto | Auto | Auto Loan |
| other | Other | Other Loan |

**Additional fields:**

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Interest Rate | Annual interest rate |
| Rate Type | Fixed or variable |
| Term (months) | Loan duration |
| Monthly Payment | Calculated from loan details (fixed-rate only) |

> [!TIP]
> For fixed-rate loans, Sure automatically calculates the monthly payment based on your original balance, interest rate, and term.

### Other Liability

Debts that don't fit the above categories:
- Personal loans from friends/family
- Medical bills
- IOUs
- Tax obligations

## Tax Treatment Explained

Investment accounts have different tax implications:

| Treatment | Description | When Taxes Apply |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Taxable** | Normal taxation | Gains taxed when realized |
| **Tax-Deferred** | Taxes postponed | Taxes paid on withdrawal |
| **Tax-Exempt** | No taxes on gains | Qualified withdrawals are tax-free |
| **Tax-Advantaged** | Special benefits | Varies by account type and use |

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Tax treatment in Sure is for informational and organizational purposes. Consult a tax professional for tax advice specific to your situation.
