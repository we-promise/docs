## About Sure

Sure is a community-maintained fork of the Maybe Finance project, which was open-sourced after the original team spent nearly $1 million building a comprehensive personal finance platform.

The app includes:
- **Account management** - Track cash, credit cards, investments, and loans
- **Transaction tracking** - Record expenses, income, and transfers
- **Investment monitoring** - Monitor your portfolio and net worth
- **Budget planning** - Set and track financial goals
- **Data ownership** - Self-host with complete control over your data
